Gig QnA Validation

Review, verify, and approve applicant QnA responses. This process acts as a pre-SOP compliance check to ensure applicants understand the gig's scope, meet eligibility requirements, and are ready to accept SOP & Payout Terms later in the flow.

SOP Validation Checklist
  • Has the applicant clearly understood the task responsibilities?
  • Do answers align with the expected scope of work?
  • Are skill claims relevant and verifiable?
  • Does the applicant acknowledge performance-based work?
  • Is the applicant suitable to proceed to SOP & Payout acceptance?
